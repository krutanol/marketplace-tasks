# Product Overview

This is a **marketplace** application — a platform that connects buyers and sellers (or providers and consumers) to exchange goods, services, or digital products.

## Core Purpose

- Enable listings/catalog browsing for available items or services
- Facilitate transactions between parties
- Manage user accounts for both sides of the marketplace (e.g., buyers and sellers/vendors)

## Key Concepts

- **Listings**: Items or services offered by sellers/providers
- **Users**: Authenticated accounts with distinct roles (buyer, seller, admin)
- **Transactions/Orders**: The exchange of value between parties
- **Reviews/Ratings**: Trust and reputation system between users
